# 👮‍♂️#SriLanka 🇱🇰 Police Press Releases `Dataset`

![LastUpdated](https://img.shields.io/badge/last_updated-2026--06--07_15:22:51-green)

[https://github.com/nuuuwan/lk_police_press_releases/tree/data/data/lk_police_press_releases](https://github.com/nuuuwan/lk_police_press_releases/tree/data/data/lk_police_press_releases)

A police press release is an official update from law enforcement on crimes, arrests, safety alerts, or public notices, ensuring transparency and public awareness.

- [**1,336** documents](https://github.com/nuuuwan/lk_police_press_releases/tree/data/data/lk_police_press_releases) (**474.7 MB**), from **2025-05-01** to **2026-06-05**, scraped from [https://www.police.lk](https://www.police.lk)

- In **JSON**, **PDF**, **TXT** & **🤗 Hugging Face**

- In **සිංහල**

- 🎓 Cite as **[arXiv:2510.04124](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.04124) [cs.CL]**
