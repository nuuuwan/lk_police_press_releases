Clarification Regarding a Video Clip Circulating on Social Media

A video clip currently circulating on social media shows a group of women making allegations that the Police conducted a house search in the Jinthupitiya area of the Coastal Police Division and making various comments regarding the incident.

In response to this matter, the Officer-in-Charge of the Colombo North Division has called for a report. According to the report received, the incident referred to in the video is related to a search operation carried out in the Jampata Street area of Colombo North on 03.03.2026.

The report further states that a raid and search operation was conducted in the above area from 4.00 p.m. to 7.30 p.m. on 03.03.2026 under the supervision of the Colombo North Divisional Officer-in-Charge. The operation was implemented with the participation of the Officers-in-Charge of all police stations in the Colombo North Division, with the objective of controlling organized criminal activities, suppressing drug trafficking, and apprehending individuals involved in such illegal activities.

It has also been revealed that several houses in this area belong to organized criminals involved in drug trafficking who are currently evading arrest while residing overseas. Accordingly, the purpose of this operation was to curb drug trafficking and organized criminal activities connected to these individuals and locations.

Furthermore, the report indicates that if any individual believes that their property or money has been misplaced, as alleged in the video, they have the right to lodge a complaint with the Police for further investigation. However, according to the information obtained so far, the allegations presented in the video clip are not accurate.
