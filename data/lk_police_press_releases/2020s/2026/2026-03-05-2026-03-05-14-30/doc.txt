Property worth Rs. 30 Million Allegedly Purchased Using Proceeds from Drug Trafficking – Illicit Assets Investigation Division

The Illicit Assets Investigation Division has commenced an investigation following a complaint regarding an organized criminal who was recently brought back to Sri Lanka from overseas. The complaint relates to a property suspected to have been purchased in the name of his daughter using proceeds generated from drug trafficking.

Accordingly, during the course of the investigation, a two-storey house and the land on which it stands, located in the Pannipitiya area and valued at approximately Rs. 30 million, is believed to have been acquired by the suspect using funds derived from narcotics trafficking.

Under the provisions of the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, the property has been ordered to be frozen for a period of seven days with effect from 05.03.2026.

Further investigations are being carried out by the Illicit Assets Investigation Division.
